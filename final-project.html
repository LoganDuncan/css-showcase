We Work Locally

With our new facility, Southern Oregon now has a local precast concrete plant. Instead of going to Eugene or Nothern California, you'll be able to get your buried infastructure needs met here at home. Better turnarounds, better customer service, and better sustainability.


Because of our location, we're able to use locally sourced materials for all of our products. With Table Rock in the background, we can mass-produce manholes or create a specifically designed custom vault, complete with rungs, safety ladders, and shelving. If it can be made, we'll make it in the Rogue Valley.

Sustainable Concrete Solutions

We are dedicated to making a product that's not only good for our customers, but goo for the enviornment as well. Our Rogue Valley facility earned SMaRT Certification, which means we've taken extra steps to make sure our products and processes are eco-friendly.


This goes beyond using only natural or raw materials and recycling waste material. Our partners were chosen specifically with sustainability in mind.
